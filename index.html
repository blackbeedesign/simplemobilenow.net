Site Intent

SimpleMobileNow.net is a cellphone sales, service and repair center in Miami. They are dealers for Simple Mobile, H20 Wireless and need a website that will represent their business online. The website must have the ability to receive the custmers payment for cellular recharge using Paypal. Their online goal is to be a first page competitor with cellular sales, services and repairs.

Goals for Redesign
  • New modern layout and design with business color scheme
  • New navigation menu with industry based names for page titles
  • New Home page layout that will be present the business services and products
  • New Service and Products page that lists them
  • Reach top page position in search engines for their sevices.
